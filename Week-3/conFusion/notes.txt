Tabs and pills

Collapse Plugin
---------------

Tooltips,Popovers and Modals
----------------------------
Revealing content upon interacting with an element on the web page.
Information displayed as an overlay
Modals
    1. More detailed information can be presented than tooltips and Popovers
    2. Modal contains header body and footer
    3. Can use the bootstrap grid in the body to organize content.
